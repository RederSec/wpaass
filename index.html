Health awareness among teenagers is crucial as it sets the foundation for a healthy adulthood. Here are some key aspects:

Mental Health

Prevalence: About 1 in 7 adolescents (10-19 years old) experience mental health condition. Common Issues: Depression, anxiety, and behavioral disorders are leading causes of illness and disability among teenagers. Risks: Factors like exposure to violence, poverty, and peer pressure can significantly impact mental health.

Physical Health

Diet and Exercise: Unhealthy diets and inactive lifestyles are major concerns. Encouraging regular physical activity and balanced nutrition is essential. Substance Use: Alcohol and tobacco use often begin in adolescence, leading to long-term health issues.
